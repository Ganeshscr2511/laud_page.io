Sridhar Vembu

The Indian Techie



Here's a time line of Mr Sridhar Vembu's life

  • 1968 - Vembu was born in 1968 in a Tamil family of farmers from a village in Thanjavur district, Tamil Nadu.
  • 1989 - completed his Electrical Engineering from the Princeton University in 1989.
  • 1994 - Vembu has a Ph.D. in electrical engineering from Princeton and started his career at Qualcomm in 1994.
  • 1996 - In 1996, Vembu, along with two of his brothers, founded a software development house for network equipment providers called AdventNet.
  • 2004 - In 2004, he set up Zoho Schools to provide vocational software development education to rural students as an alternative to formal university education. A statement from the company states that 15 to 20 percent of its engineers have no college degree, but have received vocational education from Zoho Schools. In 2020, he announced a "rural school startup" focused on free primary education.
  • 2009 - The company was renamed Zoho Corporation in 2009, focusing on providing SaaS support to Customer relationship management services.
  • 2019 - Vembu was awarded the 2019 Ernst & Young "Entrepreneur of the Year Award" in India.
  • 2020 - As of 2020, he held an 88 percent stake in the company. Forbes estimated his net worth at USD $2.44 billion.
  • 2021 - He was also the recipient of the Padma Shri, India's fourth highest civilian honor, in 2021. He was also appointed to the National Security Advisory Board (NSAB) of India in 2021.
